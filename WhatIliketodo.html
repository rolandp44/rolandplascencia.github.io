What I Like to Do

Working Out

Working out is more than just physical training for me — it’s a way to build discipline and mental strength. Whether I’m lifting weights or pushing myself through a tough workout, I see it as a way to improve not just my body, but my mindset.

Fitness has taught me consistency. Progress doesn’t happen overnight, and learning to trust the process has helped me in school, sports, and life. Every workout is a reminder that effort compounds over time.

Video Games

Video games are one of my favorite ways to relax and connect with friends. Games like ARK: Survival Evolved and ARC Raiders stand out to me because they reward teamwork, strategy, and creativity.

In ARK, I enjoy the survival aspect — gathering resources, building bases, and working together to overcome challenges. ARC Raiders takes a more tactical approach, encouraging communication and coordination in high-pressure situations.

These games aren’t just entertainment; they sharpen problem-solving skills and reinforce the importance of collaboration, leadership, and adaptability.

Why These Matter to Me

Whether I’m working out or gaming, both activities push me to grow in different ways. One builds discipline and resilience, while the other encourages creativity and teamwork. Together, they balance my life and help shape who I am.
